HUST-ASTD

The HUST-ASTD dataset is constructed by Wondium et al., from Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST). It is the first dataset for Amharic scene detection and recognition, which consists of 2,200 images from real word and SynthText.

Download from [Baidu] or [OneDrive]

Dataset Highlights

  • The first dataset in the history of Amharic Language.
  • Contains various types fonts.
  • Near 6.4 instance per image.
  • Various scenes.
