A Comprehensive Dataset and Benchmark forEthiopic/Amharic Scene Text Detection andRecognition

​

HUST-ASTD

In this work, we presented four datasets named HUST-ART, HUST-AST, ABE, and Tana for Amharic script detection and recognition in the nature scene. To the best of our knowledge, the proposed datasets are the first comprehensive and public Amharic script datasets. These datasets will promote the community to develop correlative recognition algorithms for the people who live in ETHIOPIA and some diplomats from more than 110 countries and international communities.
